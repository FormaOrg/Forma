How it comes together

From rough direction
to a site with presence

Forma takes the early spark of an idea and turns it into something structured, polished, and ready to share. Layout, imagery, typography, and tone all start aligning into a clear final experience.

01

Start with direction

Begin with a concept, a mood, or a set of references. The first step is about exploration, not perfection.

02

Shape the structure

Sections, hierarchy, spacing, and visual rhythm start to settle into place so the page feels clear and intentional.

03

Refine the final form

Colors, imagery, and details are tuned until the result feels expressive, coherent, and ready to publish.
